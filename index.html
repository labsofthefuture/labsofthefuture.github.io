5th MARCH 2019

9:00 AM - 2:00 PM


How can biotech companies benefit from the evolution of the traditional lab into a new era where cloud technologies and automation take over?

Join us for a morning of exciting talks, panels and discussion. Meet with other leading industry professionals at an event that brings together big pharma, biotech startups and academic researchers.






Featuring





Schedule

9:00 AM - 10:00 AM

REGISTRATION, REFRESHMENTS & NETWORKING

Arrive, register, meet your fellow delegates and enjoy some light refreshments - tea, coffee, croissants.

10:00 AM

Talk:
The Laboratory Of Today

Fabrice Turlais

External Sciences Lead
Cancer Research UK


What technologies and abilities do we have in the lab today? What is state-of-the-art? What are the bottlenecks and problems we currently face?

10:45 AM

Panel:
The Laboratory Of Tomorrow

Ioanna Mylonaki
Peter Crane
Beeta Balali-Mood
Andrew Barber
Fane Mensah

Where is innovation and enterprise taking us? What technologies can we expect to benefit from in the lab in the years to come?

11:30 AM

Talk:
WHAT IS A CLOUD LAB?


Anastasios Andronidis

CEO
Experoment


Can we fuse cloud technologies and laboratory automation to save time, money and get more reproducible results? How can this result in efficient scaling for biotech companies?

12:15 PM

Panel:
The future of data in the Laboratory

Matthew Higgins
Mark Warne
Anastasios Andronidis
Steven O'Connell

How will cloud infrastructure affect the amount of research data produced? What are the implications of this and how can the field benefit from the increasing trend of big data?

1:00 PM - 2:00 PM

LIGHT LUNCH & NETWORKING

Join us for a bite to eat and connect with your fellow delegates and the speakers and panellists of the conference!
